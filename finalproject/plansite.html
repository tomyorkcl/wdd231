York Skateshop - Site Plan

Site Name

York Skateshop

This name was chosen to reflect the local and authentic feel of the skateshop, with "York" evoking a sense of community and urban style.

Possible domain available: yorkskateshop.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to serve as an interactive catalog for a skateboarding shop. Users can explore featured products, add items to their cart, and easily contact the shop for collaborations or memberships. The site aims to foster a community around skateboarding.

Scenarios

  • What new products have arrived this week at York Skateshop?
  • Can I reserve or buy products directly from the site?
  • How can I get in touch with the team for a collaboration?

Color Schema

  • General background: #f4f4f4 (light gray)
  • Main color (dark text): #1a1a1a
  • Accent color: #ff6f00 (bright orange)
  • General text color: #333
  • White for boxes: #fff

The accent color is used for buttons, active links, and highlighted elements. The light background creates a contrast and maintains a clean aesthetic.
